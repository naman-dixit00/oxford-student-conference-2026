## **📌 Conference Overview**

This repository serves as a professional archive for resources, session notes, and strategic insights gathered during the **Oxford Student Conference**.

* **Conference Dates:** Monday, 16 March – Thursday, 19 March 2026.

* **Format:** Online sessions, each lasting approximately **60 minutes**.

* **Core Focus:** In-depth information regarding University courses, the collegiate system, and the undergraduate admissions process.

* **Engagement:** Live Q&A panels with University staff, academics, and current student ambassadors.



---

## **🧪 Featured Session: Studying STEM at Oxford**

* **Schedule:** Tuesday, 17 March | 10:30 PM.
* **Academic Division:** Mathematical, Physical and Life Sciences (MPLS).
* **Key Modules:**
* Comprehensive analysis of course structures and the specialized **tutorial system**.
* Practical laboratory work protocols and academic assessment frameworks.
* Exploring interdisciplinary research opportunities within the sciences.


* **Expert Panelists:** Gabriel Rigby, Sarah Baldwin, James Munro, and Helena Cotterill.

## **🌟 Specialized Programmes**

### **Opportunity Oxford**

A flagship academic initiative designed to support UK offer-holders from underrepresented backgrounds.

* **Academic Support:** Virtual preparatory events and tutor-led online courses to ensure students thrive from day one.
* **Residential Phase:** A **two-week residential programme** hosted immediately prior to the commencement of the university course.
* **Mentorship:** Ongoing guidance provided by a dedicated team of current undergraduate students.
